A builder's doctrine for vibe-coded software.

Chain in execution · Network in recovery 5 tenets
01·Tenet

Legitimacy

Argument before code.

We only grade what we can ground in objective signal. The boundary between what's measured and what's judged is documented in the work itself, not assumed.

02·Tenet

Discipline

Mapped territory before journey.

The scaffold is the work. Every surface produces builder-visible structure — checklist, claims map, decisions log — that the builder can read while the build is happening.

03·Tenet

Quality

Compute correctly. Prove it computed correctly.

We measure honestly or we don't measure. Pass-rate as distribution, not headline. Coverage measured, not declared. Cost as part of the reliability number, not a footnote.

04·Tenet

Trust

Watch the build, not just the artifact.

The audit surface is built into the build, not bolted onto the result. Sandbagging has nowhere to hide by design.

05·Tenet

Coherence

Engineered, not assumed.

Coordination is engineered. Document the seams — ask-vs-act calibration, deferrals, handoffs, where the seams live. Emergent coherence isn't coherence; it's luck.
